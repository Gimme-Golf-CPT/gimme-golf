The Concept

The World’s Best Courses,
Right Here in Cape Town.

Experience the thrill of playing a round at the world’s greatest golf courses without leaving the city. Whether you’re looking to tee off solo for a quick nine or compete with friends over 18 holes, our simulators bring legendary fairways to life with stunning precision.

For those solos looking to improve, dive into a world-class training environment. Gain an edge with professional-grade range data and training aids designed to provide instant feedback and make every practice strike count.

Gimme Golf is also the ultimate destination for families, social groups, and high-energy work functions. Beyond the fairways, enjoy our interactive skill games, premium venue hire options, and a full selection of food and drinks to keep the energy high.

Member Support

F.A.Q.

How do I book a bay?

We use the Playtomic App, but you can Check Pricing right here on our site to see our member vs. public rates before you head to the app!

Do I need my own clubs?

No. We have premium rental sets available at both locations. However, you are more than welcome to bring your own clubs (please ensure they are clean!).

What is the dress code?

Keep it casual! Unlike traditional courses, we have no strict dress code. Sneakers are perfect, but please note: no metal spikes are allowed as they damage our turf.

Can I bring guests?

Yes! Members are the heroes here—your 20% discount applies to the entire booking, so your guests play for less whenever they are with you.

What is the cancellation policy?

Life happens. You can cancel or reschedule via Playtomic up to 24 hours before your booking for a full refund to your wallet.
